Canteen

The NSUT canteen is one of the most lively spots on campus, where students gather to relax, chat, and enjoy affordable, tasty meals. It offers a wide variety of food options—from North Indian thalis and snacks to refreshing beverages—catering to everyone’s taste. The canteen’s vibrant atmosphere makes it a favorite hangout place between classes, whether for a quick bite or long conversations with friends.

Mother Dairy

The Mother Dairy area at NSUT is a popular hangout spot for students, especially in the evenings. Known for its refreshing ice creams, milkshakes, and snacks, it’s the go-to place for a quick treat after classes or a casual chat with friends. Surrounded by greenery and a relaxed vibe, the area often buzzes with laughter and conversation, making it one of the most cherished chill-out zones on campus.
